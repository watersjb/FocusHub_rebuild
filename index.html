Frequently Asked Questions

What makes FocusHub different from other productivity apps?

FocusHub isn't about tracking more tasks. It's about replacing the endless to-do list habit that ADHD/anxiety brains fall into. We limit your focus to what matters right now, use energy-based sprint timing, and provide tough-love accountability instead of gentle motivation.

Is this really designed for ADHD?

Yes. By someone with ADHD. The system accounts for: time blindness (visual timers), decision fatigue (limited buckets), task initiation paralysis (brain dump system), hyperfocus risks (break enforcement), and inconsistency (tasks persist day-to-day).

What's the "tough-love" approach?

The Agent acts like a performance coach, not a therapist. It calls out patterns, tracks your behavior, and holds you accountable. No coddling. No "you tried your best." Just: did you do the work or not?

What are the faith-grounded reflections?

Daily reflections that connect discipline and productivity to deeper purpose. They're firm and directive, drawing from virtue ethics and spiritual wisdom. Free tier has secular motivational reflections. Standard includes faith-based reflections. Premium adds multi-faith options.

Can I use this without paying?

Yes. Lite tier is completely free and includes all core features: timer, task management, grading, reflections. Standard and Premium add celebrations, pattern tracking, and advanced features.

Does my data stay private?

Everything runs in your browser. Your tasks, grades, and reflections are stored locally on your device. We don't collect or see your data. Future cloud sync will be opt-in only.

What if I miss a day?

The system is built for this. Tasks persist until you complete them. Your streak resets, but your data doesn't disappear. The Agent will call you out, but you can start fresh immediately. No guilt spirals.

How do I get a license code for Standard/Premium?

Purchase links coming soon. For now, launch the free version and see if the system works for you. Paid tiers will unlock via license code entry in the app.

2. Brain Dump Your Tasks

  • Tasks go to the Holding Area first

3. Sort Into Strategic Buckets

4. Run Your Sprints

Hit START and focus on one task. When the timer ends:

  • Take the suggested break (5 min short, 15 min long after 4 sprints)
  • Or skip it and keep going (but the Agent will judge you)
  • After 2 hours without a break, you'll be forced to stop
Pro tip: The tab will blink and notify you when time's up. Works even in background tabs.

5. Log Distractions (Don't Fight Them)

When a thought derails you, capture it immediately:

  • Click "Log Distraction"
  • Write what pulled you away
  • Convert to task if needed
  • Get back to work
Pro tip: The Agent tracks your distraction count. More than 7 in a day? You'll hear about it.

6. Watch the Agent (Your Accountability GPS)

The Agent isn't a cheerleader. It's a performance coach that tracks:

  • Your daily pace (sprints completed vs. 8-sprint target)
  • Pattern recognition (task switching, late-night work)
  • Time-aware messaging (calls out poor planning)
  • Real-time feedback on your behavior
Pro tip: If you're working past 10pm, the Agent will tell you this is poor time management, not hustle.

7. End Your Day & Get Graded

Click "END DAY" when you're done. You'll get an A-F grade based on:

  • Sprints completed (target: 8)
  • Tasks finished
  • Distractions logged

Your grade is saved to the Progress Tracker. Watch your consistency over time.

  • Combat the "I got nothing done" feeling
  • Build momentum day by day
